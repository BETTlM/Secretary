Privacy Policy for DONNA AI

Effective Date: November 5, 2025

Welcome to Donna AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address (via Google or direct sign-up) and your name. We also ask for your WhatsApp phone number to link your account to the bot.
  • User Content: We process the content of the WhatsApp messages you forward to our service. This is necessary to parse event details.
  • API Tokens & Keys: To sync your events, we require you to provide an API Key for Notion and an OAuth Refresh Token for Google Calendar.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide the Service: We use your phone number to identify you. We use your API tokens to create events in your Google Calendar and pages in your Notion database on your behalf.
  • To Parse Events: The text content of your forwarded WhatsApp messages is sent to a third-party AI service (Google Gemini) for the sole purpose of extracting event details (title, deadline, priority).
  • To Send Reminders: We store the event title, deadline, and your phone number in our `scheduled_events` database to send you a WhatsApp reminder one hour before the event.

3. How We Store and Secure Your Information

  • Your Google Calendar and Notion API tokens are **highly sensitive**. We store them securely in our encrypted Supabase database.
  • We **never** log or store the full content of your WhatsApp messages after they have been processed. We only store the extracted event details (title, deadline) required for the reminder.
  • All communication between you, our server, and third-party APIs is encrypted using industry-standard SSL (HTTPS).

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with any third parties, with two critical exceptions:

  • Google Gemini: The *text* of your message is sent to the Gemini API for parsing. Your personal data (phone number, email, API keys) is **never** sent to the AI.
  • WhatsApp (Meta API): We use the Meta API to send confirmation and reminder messages back to your phone number.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access and delete your data at any time.

  • You can revoke Google Calendar access at any time from your Google Account settings.
  • You can rotate or delete your Notion API key from your Notion settings.
  • To delete your entire account and all associated data (including API keys and scheduled reminders), please contact us at **[YOUR_CONTACT_EMAIL_HERE]**.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
